# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity,
gender identity and expression, level of experience, education,
socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or
sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward others.
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the community and product quality.
- Taking responsibility for mistakes and learning from them.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- The use of sexualized language or imagery.
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of
acceptable behavior and may take corrective action in response to behavior they
deem inappropriate or harmful.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within project spaces and in public spaces when an
individual is representing the project.

## Enforcement

Report incidents to project maintainers through repository communication
channels. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.

## Enforcement Guidelines

Maintainers may apply these community impact guidelines:

- Correction: Clarify expected behavior.
- Warning: Formal notice for violation.
- Temporary Ban: Time-limited loss of interaction privileges.
- Permanent Ban: Ongoing removal from community participation.
